About Us

Peter Luketa
Managing Director

Peter Luketa is a senior banker with over 40 years' experience at Midland Bank, Midland Montagu and HSBC Investment Bank and HSBC Bank plc. Peter has spent the last twenty years in Project and Export Finance, with 12 years as Managing Director and 7 years as Global Head of Export Finance and Forfaiting. Peter led HSBC Export Finance to the number one position in its industry and has senior level contacts with Public Sector, Corporates, Financial Institutions as well as Private Banking. He currently sits on the Board of Anglo-Gulf Trade Bank, a digitally based trade finance bank based in the ADGM (Abu Dhabi). The two main shareholders are Mubadala and the private Rowland family. He is also senior consultant to Cranmore Partners, Abu Dhabi, CC Solutions, New York and International Hospitals Group, Bucks UK. He has a specialist focus on MENA, Russia and Eastern Europe.

Javier Letamendi
Senior Director

Experienced trade and export finance banker based in the Iberian Peninsula. Recently, Javi has developed a number of renewable energy and real estate projects in Spain bringing and concluding both equity and debt finance solutions. He is an expert originating opportunities whether in Spain or Latin America and he has close contacts with many European energy developers, equity providers and financial institutions on both sides of the Atlantic. He was previously with BBVA in London, Arab Banking Corporation and as Senior Director of HSBC Project and Export Finance in Spain. Fluent in English, Portuguese, Italian as well as his native Spanish.

Neven Luketa
